FunAI research Lab

Welcome! We are the Fundamental AI research Lab (FunAI Lab) at the University of Technology Nuremberg (UTN), directed by Prof. Dr. Yuki M. Asano. We conduct fundamental AI research with high impact. We develop core technologies of current Foundation Models:

  • Self-supervised learning
  • Vision-language and multimodal learning
  • LLM reasoning and planning
  • Post-pretraining and instruction tuning

We put the fun into fundamental research.
